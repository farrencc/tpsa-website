About Us

The Problem Solving Association is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving public services through the application of mathematics and technology. Our team of experts works tirelessly to develop innovative solutions for complex societal challenges. Through our commitment to open-source development and transparent methodology, we aim to create lasting positive change in electoral systems, crisis management, and educational access.

Our Projects

Algorithm-Based, Provably Fair Electoral Redistricting

We have developed an algorithm for electoral redistricting in Ireland based on a physics model used for condensed matter systems (the Potts model). Constitutional objectives are encoded into an “energy” description, where we then using advanced numerical techniques to find optimal electoral boundary configurations. This first-principles method processes in minutes what takes a year by hand. This approach can explore vastly more boundary configurations than a human could. With this transparent, lean algorithm for redistricting, we can offer quantifiable, consistent, auditable application of constitutional objectives. We are adamant on eliminating any potential for gerrymandering. Providing aim to provide the public the means to verify the boundaries. All the while, we retain the ability to incorporate key inter-community relationships and population projections.

Introduction to AI for Charities

Charities are a valuable stakeholder in ensuring rule of law and social justice values are embedded in the AI policy-making process. Our introductory session informs and empowers charities to actively take part in policy discussions advocating for the values they espouse and the groups they represent. The session introduces deep learning models with special emphasis on large language models, the EU AI Act and relevant EU and Irish authorities, the risks AI can pose, and the benefits charities themselves can derive from ethical AI use - with particular emphasis on grant writing.

Tracking Political Advertising Compliance

The tensions that arise in a paradigm of both 'transparency' and 'self-regulation' require independent verifications of compliance to take place. This project assesses the extent to which the spend figures, disclaimers, and sources of funds for online political advertising are compliant with existing regulation, and identifies gaps in existing regulation practice.

The Ecnetica Project

The Ecnetica project exists to induce a paradigm shift in Irish education by tearing down the barriers that have turned advanced mathematical understanding into a privilege rather than a right. Through cutting-edge animations, expert instruction, and freely accessible content, we’re dismantling the pay-to- win culture of private tuition while simultaneously raising the standard of mathematics education across the nation.

Our mission transcends exam preparation – we aim to ignite a deep, intuitive understanding of math- ematics that will empower the next generation of Irish problem solvers. We’re building a future where understanding of Higher Level mathematics is democratised, where every student can develop the quantitative reasoning skills needed to tackle tomorrow’s challenges, and where Ireland’s tradition of academic excellence is accessible to all. This is mathematics education reimagined: comprehensive, inspired, and absolutely free
