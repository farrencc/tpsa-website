    Our Committee

    We hold our annual general meeting, AGM, at the end of the academic year, sometime early April. At our AGMs students are able to run for one of the 17 available positions. Please refer to our constitution for more information on each role.

    Artem

    Auditor

    The role of Auditor shall encompass substantial discretion in the pursuit of specific objectives and strategies of the TPSA, contingent upon the possession of considerable initiative, motivation, and determination.

    Caoimhe

    Secretary

    The Secretary shall be responsible for ensuring that meetings are organized and documented appropriately, maintaining effective administration, and disseminating updates.

    Daniel

    Treasurer

    The Treasurer shall be responsible for the financial management of the association.

    Eden

    PRO

    The PROs shall have significant influence on the public perception of the association among students, academics, and industry professionals.

    Conor

    PRO

    The PROs shall have significant influence on the public perception of the association among students, academics, and industry professionals.

    Cian

    Webmaster

    The Webmaster shall be responsible for maintaining and updating the TPSA website.

    Ameilia

    Video Coordinator

    The VC shall be particularly active during the summer months, overseeing the recruitment, recording, editing, and publication of student-run lecture series.

    Cas

    Seminar Director

    The Seminar Director shall oversee the weekly seminars, including the booking of lecture theatres and ensuring their responsible use. Historically, this role included organizing a teacher roster to ensure consistent seminar leadership.

    Vedanth

    IOP Rep

    The IOP Representative shall represent the IOP within the TPSA. This role involves applying for annual funding from the IOP, receiving emails from the IOP, and forwarding pertinent information to the committee.

    Aidan

    PLANCKS Captain

    The PLANCKS Captain shall assemble and train a team of skilled problem solvers for the PLANCKS competition. This individual should possess strong problem-solving skills and leadership capabilities.

    Ed

    Outreach Officer

    The Outreach Officer shall devise strategies to engage the broader college community and general public with theoretical physics.

    Alen

    Administrative Officer

    The Administrative Officer shall assist with administrative tasks within the committee.

    Max

    Maths Rep

    The Mathematics Rep shall be elected from the respective student body. The sole requirement for this role is that the representative must be a student of the relevant discipline. These representatives shall communicate pertinent information to their respective courses and represent their courses in committee meetings.

    Genna

    Physics Rep

    The Physics Rep shall be elected from the respective student body. The sole requirement for this role is that the representative must be a student of the relevant discipline. These representatives shall communicate pertinent information to their respective courses and represent their courses in committee meetings.

    TBD

    First Year Rep

    The purpose of the First-Year Representative is dynamic and subject to change given the year and wishes of the present committee. Fundamentally, the purpose of the First-Year Representative is to ensure the First-Year students ample access and attendance to TPSA events particularly seminars.

    Seán

    Postgrad Rep

    The Postgraduate Representative shall be elected from the respective student body. This representative shall communicate relevant information to postgraduate students, represent postgraduate interests in committee meetings, and encourage further participation among the postgraduate student body, acting as a link between the committee and the postgraduate students.

    Maria

    Queens Rep

    The Queen's Representative shall be elected from the respective student body. The sole requirement for this role is that the representative must be a student with affiliation to Queen's University Belfast. This representative shall communicate relevant information to students and represent Queen's-related interests in committee meetings.
