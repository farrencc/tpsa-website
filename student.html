    Our Vision

    Here at the TPSA we are dedicated to fostering a community across the island of Ireland excited about theoretical physics. We are committed to providing our members, whether they are studying theoretical physics or an aligned subject, a space in which to engage and grow as academics. We aspire to provide them with the opportunities and curiosities to succeed in our field. We wish to actively engage with the physics and mathematics communities across all of Ireland to create a united and robust network.

    Our Actions

    Across the year we hold various events and talks, offering students a glimpse into the wider world of theoretical physics.

    Check out our calendar for more information on upcoming events.

    • Weekly collaborative seminars
    • Talks given by experts in their respective fields
    • Our yearly internship & post-grad talks
    • Foundation Scholarship exam preparation seminars
    • Workshops sharpening skills such as LaTeX, Mathematica & Python
    • Our annual hackathon in September
    • Events connecting the theoretical physics community
